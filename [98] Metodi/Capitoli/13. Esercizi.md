- Es. 1 Errore totale - Differenza di quadrati
    
    Calcolare l'errore totale nel calcolo
    
    $y=f(x_1,x_2)= x_1^2-x_2^2$
    
    Si può procedere in 2 modi:
    
    ==Algoritmo 1 Algoritmo 2==  
      
    $z^1=x_1x_1$﻿ $z^1=x_1-x_2$﻿  
      
    $z^2=x_2x_2$﻿ $z^2=x_1+x_2$﻿  
      
    $z^3=z^1-z^2$﻿ $z^3=z^1z^2$﻿
    
    - ==Algoritmo 1==
        
        $\epsilon_{TOT}^1\approx\gamma_1+c_1^1\epsilon_{R_1}+c_2^1\epsilon_{R_1}=\gamma_1+2\epsilon_{R_1}$
        
        $\epsilon_{TOT}^2\approx\gamma_2+c_1^2\epsilon_{R_2}+c_2^2\epsilon_{R_2}=\gamma_2+2\epsilon_{R_2}$
        
        > [!important]  
        > Per la moltiplicazione sappiamo che l'indice di condizionamento è 1, Quindi @import url('https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/KaTeX/0.16.9/katex.min.css')c11=c21=c12=c22=1c_1^1=c_2^1=c_1^2=c_2^2=1c11​=c21​=c12​=c22​=1﻿  
        
        $\epsilon_{TOT}=\epsilon_{TOT}^3\approx \gamma_3+c_1^3\epsilon_{TOT}^1+c_2^3\epsilon_{TOT}^2=\\=\gamma_3+\frac{z^1}{z^1-z^2}\epsilon_{TOT}^1+\frac{-z^2}{z^1-z^2}\epsilon_{TOT}^2=\\=\gamma_3+\frac{z^1}{z^1-z^2}(\gamma_1+2\epsilon_{R_1})+\frac{-z^2}{z^1-z^2}+(\gamma_1+2\epsilon_{R_2})=\\=\gamma_3+\frac{z^1}{z^1-z^2}\gamma_1-\frac{z^2}{z^1-z^2}\gamma_2+\frac{2z^1}{z^1-z^2}\epsilon_{R_1}+\frac{-2z^2}{z^1-z^2}\epsilon_{R_2}=$
        
        $=\gamma_3+\frac{x^2_1}{x^2_1-x^2_2}\gamma_1+\frac{-x^2_2}{x^2_1-x^2_2}\gamma_2+\frac{2x^2_1}{x^2_1-x^2_2}\epsilon_{R_1}+\frac{-2x^2_1}{x^2_1-x^2_2}\epsilon_{R_2}$
        
        > [!important]  
        > I primi 3 addendi rappresentano l'errore algoritmico, mentre gli altri 2 l'errore inerente.     - ==Algoritmo 2==
        
        $\epsilon_{TOT}^1\approx\gamma_1+c_1^1\epsilon_{R_1}+c_2^1\epsilon_{R_2}=\gamma_1+\frac{x_1}{x_1-x_2}\epsilon_{R_1}+\frac{-x_2}{x_1-x_2}\epsilon_{R_2}$
        
        $\epsilon_{TOT}^2\approx\gamma_2+c_1^2\epsilon_{R_1}+c_2^2\epsilon_{R_2}=\gamma_2+\frac{x_1}{x_1+x_2}\epsilon_{R_1}+\frac{x_2}{x_1+x_2}\epsilon_{R_2}$
        
        $\epsilon_{TOT}=\epsilon_{TOT}^3\approx\gamma_3+c_1^3\epsilon_{TOT}^1+c_2^3\epsilon_{TOT}^2=\gamma_3+\epsilon_{TOT}^1+\epsilon_{TOT}^2=\\\footnotesize=\gamma_3+\bigg(\gamma_1+\frac{x_1}{x_1-x_2}\epsilon_{R_1}+\frac{-x_2}{x_1-x_2}\epsilon_{R_2}\bigg)+\bigg(\gamma_2+\frac{x_1}{x_1+x_2}\epsilon_{R_1}+\frac{x_2}{x_1+x_2}\epsilon_{R_2}\bigg)=\\$
