### Limiti notevoli successioni

1.

$$
\text{Sia }a\in\mathbb{R}, \lim\limits_{n\to\infty}a^n=\begin{cases}
+\infty,\quad se\ a>1\\
1,\qquad\ se\ a=1\\
0,\qquad\ se\ |a|<1\\
\nexists,\qquad\ se\ a\le1
\end{cases}
$$

2.

$$
\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{\alpha_0+\alpha_1n+\cdots+\alpha_tn^t}{\beta_0+\beta_1n+\cdots+\beta_rn^r}=\begin{cases}
0,\qquad\ se\ r<t\\
+\infty,\quad se\ t>r,\ \alpha_t>0\\
-\infty,\quad se\ t>r,\ \alpha_t<0\\
\frac{\alpha_t}{\beta_r},\qquad se\ t=r
\end{cases}
$$

3.

$$
\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\sqrt[n]{n^b}=1\qquad b\in\mathbb{N}
$$

4.

$$
\text{Se }a_n\longrightarrow0,\ \lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\sin(a_n)=0\text{ e } \lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\cos(a_n)=1
$$

5.

$$
\text{Se }a_n\longrightarrow0,\ a_n\ne0,\ \lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\frac{\sin(a_n)}{a_n}=1
$$

6.

$$
\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\bigg(1+\frac{1}{n}\bigg)^n=e
$$

### Limiti notevoli funzioni

$$
\lim\limits_{x\to\pm\infty}\bigg(1+\frac{1}{x}\bigg)^x=e\qquad\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=1
$$

$$
\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{1-\cos x}{x^2}=\frac{1}{2}\qquad\lim\limits_{x\to0}\frac{1-\cos x}{x}=0
$$
